## Short value proposition

Solomon gives you **production-ready conversational AI** without hiring a platform team. ## Starting pricing & engagement model

Pricing reflects **your own dedicated environment**, **professional go-live**, and **ongoing management**—not a per-seat toy app.

**Illustrative starting point — “Dedicated managed chat” (Base)**  
- **One-time implementation:** from **about $8,500** (typical range **$5,000 – $15,000** depending on complexity)  
- **Ongoing management:** from **about $1,900 per month** (typical range **$1,200 – $2,800** per month)  

**What that commonly includes:** one **dedicated Solomon environment** for your organization; typically **one live site and a staging/testing setup**; **up to two outbound connection points** (e.g. webhook destinations) for your systems; standard go-live support; **email-based support** with next-business-day responsiveness for covered issues.

**Larger or multi-brand needs** — more sites, deeper integrations, faster support, or formal uptime commitments — move to **Pro** or **Enterprise** tiers with **custom quotes**. Example reference packages in our model land around **~$22,000** setup and **~$4,200**/month for richer multi-brand and integration scope.

**AI usage:** In most engagements, **you maintain your own account with the AI provider** (e.g. OpenAI) and pay their usage directly. **Our fees cover the Solomon service, operations, and support**—not your token usage.

Final numbers depend on your **exact footprint**, **integrations**, and **support level**—we confirm everything in a short discovery call and a written proposal.

## What implementation looks like

1. **Discovery** — We confirm your sites, goals, integrations, and who owns AI billing.  
2. **Agreement** — Clear scope: what’s dedicated to you, what’s included, how we support you.  
3. **Go-live** — We stand up **your environment**, connect your assistant, verify readiness, and walk your team through what they need to know.  
4. **Operate** — We maintain the service per your tier: patches, monitoring discipline, and agreed support channels.  
5. **Evolve** — Add internal brands, adjust integrations, or upgrade tiers as you grow—scoped so expectations stay clear.

You are never asked to “figure out the backend” on your own under the **standard managed offer**.
